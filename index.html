About Us

We are The Bladerunners, a passionate robotics team competing in the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC). FTC is an international robotics competition that challenges students to design, build, and program robots to compete in exciting, head-to-head matches. Our mission goes beyond building a robot – we strive to develop teamwork, creativity, and problem-solving skills while sharing the excitement of STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Mathematics) with our community. By combining innovation with collaboration, we aim not only to perform on the competition field but also to inspire others to explore the world of technology and engineering.
